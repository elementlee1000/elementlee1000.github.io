Element Lee (Yifan Li) is a Vienna-based Chinese digital artist. He works from an autonomous aesthetic to sense, think, and render the world. By employing pixels and characters as the fundamental units of the digital age, he continues the tradition of line drawing while expanding the boundaries of drawing. His practice seeks a condition in which meaning exceeds language and foregrounds the interconnection between works.

MA: Digital Arts at the University of Applied Arts Vienna (die Angewandte).
BA: Art Theory, China Central Academy of Fine Arts.
